Common Question

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.

How can I book a car for rental with Ghumo?

You can book a car for rental with Ghumo by visiting our website or contacting our office directly.

What types of cars are available for rental at Ghumo?

Ghumo offers a variety of cars for rental including sedans, SUVs, and luxury vehicles.

Is there a minimum age requirement to rent a car from Ghumo?

Yes, the minimum age requirement to rent a car from Ghumo is 21 years old.

Yes, Ghumo offers the flexibility to return the rental car to a different location for an additional fee.

What should I do in case of an emergency while using a Ghumo rental car?

In case of an emergency while using a Ghumo rental car, please contact our 24/7 customer support hotline for assistance.
